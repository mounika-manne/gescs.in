About Us

Geospatial, Environmental & Structural Consulting Services (GESCS) is a specialized consulting firm delivering high-precision data, scientific expertise, and engineering insight to support infrastructure development, environmental planning, and structural engineering projects.

Our team includes PhD-level experts and industry professionals with advanced knowledge in geospatial technologies, environmental sciences, and structural engineering. We integrate research-driven methodologies with practical field experience to ensure accuracy, compliance, and long-term project sustainability.

Our Core Competencies

Geospatial Services

We provide end-to-end geospatial solutions using advanced surveying, GIS, and remote-sensing tools. Our capabilities include:

  • Topographic and cadastral surveys
  • GIS development and spatial analytics
  • Remote sensing and terrain modeling
  • Land-use planning and resource mapping

Environmental Consulting

Our environmental division delivers science-driven assessments aligned with national and international environmental standards. Key services include:

  • Environmental Impact Assessments (EIA)
  • Environmental audits and compliance reporting
  • Baseline environmental and ecological studies
  • Pollution monitoring and resource management

Structural Engineering

Our PhD-trained structural engineers offer reliable and innovative engineering solutions designed for safety and longevity. Our expertise covers:

  • Structural design and analysis
  • Structural health monitoring
  • Risk and stability assessments
  • Rehabilitation and retrofit strategies

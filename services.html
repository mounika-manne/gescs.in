Our Services

At Geospatial, Environmental & Structural Consulting Services (GESCS), we provide high-quality, research-driven, and technically precise services delivered by experienced professionals and PhD-level experts. Our service offerings span three core domains:

Geospatial Services

Our geospatial division uses advanced surveying instruments, GIS technology, and remote sensing tools to deliver accurate spatial data and mapping solutions.

  • Topographic Surveys
  • Cadastral & Boundary Mapping
  • GIS Database Creation & Spatial Analysis
  • Remote Sensing & Terrain Interpretation
  • Digital Elevation Modeling & Landform Mapping

Environmental Consulting

We provide end-to-end environmental assessment and compliance services aligned with state, national, and international environmental standards.

  • Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA)
  • Environmental Audits & Compliance Studies
  • Baseline Environmental & Ecological Surveys
  • Water, Soil, and Air Quality Monitoring
  • Waste Management & Pollution Control Planning

Structural Engineering Services

Led by PhD-qualified structural engineers, our structural division specializes in delivering scientifically optimized, safe, and cost-effective engineering solutions.

  • Structural Design & Analysis
  • Structural Health Monitoring & Assessment
  • Risk, Load, and Stability Evaluations
  • Retrofit & Rehabilitation Strategies
  • Design Review & Quality Assurance

  • Accuracy, reliability, and data-driven decision-making
  • Compliance-focused and sustainability-oriented approach
  • End-to-end consultancy across multiple engineering domains
  • Client-centric service delivery and transparent communication
